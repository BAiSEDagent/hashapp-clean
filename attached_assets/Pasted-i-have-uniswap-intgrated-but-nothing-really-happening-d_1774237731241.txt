The natural integration:

Right now when a human approves a spend request, the delegation executes a USDC transfer directly. But what if the human's wallet has ETH and not enough USDC? Or what if the agent wants to pay in USDC but the treasury is in WETH?

Uniswap becomes the settlement layer:

Agent sends spend_request for $5 USDC
        ↓
Human approves
        ↓
Hashapp checks: does wallet have enough USDC?
        ↓
If not — Uniswap swaps ETH → USDC automatically
        ↓
Delegation spend executes with the swapped USDC
        ↓
Receipt shows: swap tx + spend tx + Venice attribution
That is "agents that pay" with Uniswap as the actual settlement layer. Not a swap interface bolted on the side. Uniswap is why the spend can happen at all.
